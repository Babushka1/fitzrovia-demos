Design Principles

  • Yardi stays the system of record. This layer adds what Voyager doesn't: cross-source sentiment fusion, resident-level recovery, bespoke portfolio views.
  • Human in the loop. Every AI action (dispatch, concession, outreach) requires PM approval; overrides train the model.
  • Privacy by design. Canadian data residency; review-to-resident matches marked "probable" until human-confirmed; PIPEDA & Quebec Law 25 compliant.
  • Service recovery, not special treatment. Interventions trigger on verified service failures (SLA breach, repeat fault) — never on complaint volume alone.

Why This Creates Value

  • Renewals: each recovered point of renewal at a 316-unit community ≈ $41k/yr (turn cost + vacancy + spread).
  • Maintenance: triage automation cut median resolve time 3.4 → 2.1 days in modeled scenario; early asset intervention avoids 5-figure emergency repairs.
  • Reputation: Google rating is the top organic leasing channel; systematic review response + recovery lifts it measurably.
  • Brand: operationalizes the Fitzrovia Standard — service quality becomes measured, not anecdotal.
